ECO-TRONICS MISSION BRIEFING

ECO-TRONICS is a national-level technical hackathon of the Department of ECE, SRM IST Ramapuram. It stands as a pinnacle of innovation and engineering excellence, providing a dynamic platform for students to showcase their talent across diverse domains of electronics and communication.

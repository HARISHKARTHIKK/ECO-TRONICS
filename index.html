MISSION RULES & ENGAGEMENT GUIDELINES // VER 2026.02

01

ELIGIBILITY

Open exclusively to engineering undergraduate students. Participation is strictly team-based.

02

TEAM FORMATION

Each team must consist of exactly 3 members. Participants may join only one team, and all members must complete registration before the deadline.

03

PPT PREPARATION

Teams must prepare their PPT strictly using the format provided by the organisers in the format TEAM_NAME.pdf

  • Problem Statement
  • Proposed Solution
  • Technical Approach
  • SDG Impact
  • Feasibility
04

HACKATHON FORMAT

The event will be conducted as a 24-hour continuous physical hackathon. Teams must develop their prototype within the event duration and remain present throughout.

05

PROJECT RULES

  • Projects must be original and built entirely during the hackathon.
  • Teams must work only on the provided problem statements.
  • All projects must align with the relevant SDGs.
06

PAYMENT CONFIRMATION

After successful payment, participants will receive a confirmation email containing their Team ID.

07

EVALUATION CRITERIA

  • Innovation & Technical Execution
  • SDG Impact & Feasibility
  • Quality of Final Presentation
08

COMPLIANCE

Any rule violation, use of pre-built projects, or failure to meet requirements will result in immediate disqualification. Judges‚Äô and organizers‚Äô decisions will be final.
